A registered non-profit organization based in Vrygrond, Cape Town, serving as a holistic community development center offering access to education, health support, food security, and empowerment opportunities.

Our Mission: Uplift and empower vulnerable individuals and families by addressing the root causes of poverty, crime, and unemployment through integrated programs and outreach services.

The Challenges We Face

Vrygrond - the oldest informal settlement in the Western Cape - faces significant challenges. But where others see problems, we see opportunities for transformation.
